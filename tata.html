TATA NEXON


price = 15 LAKH

The Nexon made its debut as a prototype exhibited at the New Delhi Motor Show in 2014. The final model was presented in 2016. It is Tata's first crossover SUV.


TATA ALTROZ


price = 11 lakh

The Tata Altroz is a subcompact hatchback manufactured by Tata Motors. It was launched on 22 January 2020. The Altroz was revealed at the 89th Geneva International Motor Show alongside the new Buzzard, Buzzard Sport, and H2X subcompact SUV concept.


TATA HARRIER


price = 25 lakh

The Tata Harrier is a five-seater compact SUV produced by the Indian automaker Tata Motors. It was launched in Indian market on 23 January 2019 and is positioned between the subcompact Tata Nexon and the mid-size Tata Hexa.


TATA TIAGO


price = 8 lakh

The Tata Tiago is a city car made by Tata Motors in India since 2016. It is derived from the Tata Kite project. The Tiago was previously announced as the Tata Zica, with "Zica" short for "zippy car", but it was changed because the launch of the car coincided with the outbreak of Zika virus. Tiago, a common Portuguese masculine name, was picked from suggestions solicited online.


TATA SAFARI STORME


price = 20 lakh

The Tata Safari is a mid-size SUV produced by the Indian automobile manufacturer Tata Motors since 1998.


TATA HEXA


price = 20 lakh

The Tata Hexa is a mid-size (J-segment) SUV manufactured by Indian passenger and commercial vehicles manufacturer Tata Motors. The SUV, which was originally unveiled in the 2016 Geneva Motor Show, was launched into the Indian markets in January 2017. The Tata Hexa is based on the company's previously used Aria platform, with a body-on-frame chassis.


TATA TIGOR


price = 9 lakh

The Tata Tigor is a subcompact sedan manufactured by Tata Motors in India based on the Tata Tiago hatchback. It was launched in India in March 2017.
