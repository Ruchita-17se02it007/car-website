ROLLS-ROYCE GHOST


price = 7 cr

The Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury car manufactured by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. The "Ghost" nameplate, named in honour of the Silver Ghost, a car first produced in 1906, was announced in April 2009 at the Auto Shanghai show. The production model was officially unveiled at the 2009 Frankfurt Motor Show. The Ghost Extended Wheelbase was introduced in 2011.


ROLLS-ROYCE CULLINAN


price = 10 cr

The Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a full-sized luxury SUV produced by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. The Cullinan is the first SUV to be launched by the Rolls-Royce marque, and is also the brand's first all-wheel drive vehicle.
